Our Services

COUNSELLING SERVICES

The organizationspromotes an eclectic/integrative approach to counseling base on a humanistic model of counseling and training. In both counseling and training great care is taken to respect and enhance the autonomy of our clients, trainees and supervisors all this is derived from principles of character, competence to the community on global perspective. Effectiveness and efficiency is what is valued most.

RESOURCE CENTER

The organizations have Resource Centres whose main purpose shall be to work with relevant bodies to disseminate the right information in regards to thorny issues like HIV, Non-Communicable Disease (NCDs) and lifestyle diseases diabetics, ulcers and high blood pressure. The Resource Centre shall also organise several work-shops, retreats, training sensitisation and capacity building in the community and not limited to work places in Cop-rate and government institutions.

THERAPY
