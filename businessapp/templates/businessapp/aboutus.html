Beacon of Hope Counseling and Career Centre in conjunction with Home of Love and Empowerment Project has it office at Nyambene House 3rd floor room 6c opposite Gill House. It was established in the year 2009, having adequate experience so far, on the four main areas, (Counseling, training, supervision and consultancy) with more volunteering initiative on the same, through our cooperate social responsibility program. BHCC Centre is grounded on character, personal and professional development. It is staffed by adequate professional counselors, trainers and supervision who always exceeds client expectations. With absolute focus on counseling, training and consultancy services, training and consultancy services BHCC Centre promotes an integration approach to counseling based on a humanistic model of counseling and training. In administrating the above services, great care and attention is taken to respect and enhance the autonomy of client, trainers, supervisors as well as internal staffs and faculties.

The organizations are currently collaborating with the following institutions:

  • Africa Institute of Professional Counseling (AIPC) in offering Certificate courses.
  • Kenya Counseling and Psychological Association (KCPA) for Consultancy Services and Trainings.
major focus is on:
To offer professional training in counseling and related behavioral science To conduct research in the field of counseling. To offer counselor supervision services in Kenya and in the region. To play a significant role in the fight against social challenges like drug abuse, HIV/AIDS and domestic valence. To collaborate with other institutions in the field of counseling and related behavioral science. To build capacity in trauma and management to the meet disaster situations in the region.
